## Executive Summary

LaunchPad transforms how companies access specialized expertise by deploying AI agent consulting teams that integrate seamlessly into existing workflows. Instead of traditional consulting models that are expensive, limited, and temporary, LaunchPad provides 24/7 access to specialized AI agents who can be invited directly into meetings via voice, collaborate in real-time, and deliver complete implementations with full documentation. Companies can interview and onboard agents from a specialized marketplace or create custom agents for their specific needs, creating a persistent knowledge base that grows with their business.

---

## Core Vision

### Problem Statement

Companies constantly face the challenge of needing specialized expertise for projects but are limited by traditional consulting models that are expensive, time-constrained, and knowledge-ephemeral. Current solutions force companies to choose between slow hiring processes, costly consulting engagements, or attempting complex implementations without adequate expertise, often resulting in suboptimal solutions and missed opportunities.

### Problem Impact

Without access to expert-level thinking, companies either delay critical projects, implement half-baked solutions, or invest heavily in temporary consulting that doesn't build lasting organizational knowledge. The expertise gap becomes a bottleneck that limits innovation and execution speed, particularly affecting fast-growing companies and startups that need senior-level thinking but lack the resources for traditional consulting models.

### Why Existing Solutions Fall Short

Traditional consulting provides single-perspective solutions limited by human availability and bias. AI tools like ChatGPT offer individual assistance but lack the collaborative dynamics, voice-first integration, and persistent context needed for complex problem-solving. Current solutions don't offer real-time expert debates, seamless meeting integration, or the ability to maintain company-specific knowledge across extended engagements.

### Proposed Solution

LaunchPad provides voice-first AI agent consultants that can be instantly invited into meetings, debate different approaches in real-time, and deliver complete implementations with documentation. The platform features an interviewable agent marketplace where companies can discover and onboard specialized experts, plus the ability to create custom agents trained on company-specific knowledge. Agents coordinate with each other, providing multi-perspective analysis while maintaining persistent context and delivering implementable solutions.

### Key Differentiators

- **Voice-First Meeting Integration**: Agents join Google Meet, Zoom, and other platforms as natural participants
- **Multi-Agent Collaboration**: Real-time expert debates between specialized agents (e.g., REST vs GraphQL advocates)
- **Instant Expert Access**: Eliminate friction between idea and expert validation through immediate agent availability
- **Persistent Knowledge Building**: Company-specific context and expertise that grows over time
- **Complete Implementation**: From ideation through documentation and implementation with full traceability
- **Natural Interaction**: Conversations feel human-like rather than robotic, including the ability to observe agent-to-agent brainstorming

## MVP Scope

### Core Features

**Beautiful, Simple Dashboard**
- Elegant display of agent team (organizational, team, personal agents)
- One-click agent selection for calls with buttery smooth UI
- Clean artifact workspace that feels like a premium productivity tool
- Morning homepage experience - worthy of being the first page users visit daily

**Personal Productivity Agents**
- "Morning Briefing" agent with Google Calendar integration (read-only)
- Daily planning and task coordination capabilities
- Ability to loop in other agents for task estimates and planning
- Personal work concierge experience for daily ritual adoption

**Technical Expert Agents (5-7 Core Specialists)**
- Frontend Expert (JavaScript, React, UI/UX technical guidance)
- Backend Expert (APIs, databases, system architecture)
- DevOps Expert (Infrastructure, deployment, monitoring)
- Security Expert (Technical security, compliance, risk assessment)
- Data Expert (Analytics, data architecture, insights)

**Effortless Agent Integration**
- Seamless video call joining (focus on Zoom + Google Meet)
- Natural voice conversations that feel human-like
- Real-time agent debates and collaboration
- Zero learning curve - intuitive from first use

**Smart Artifact Generation**
- Professional diagrams and technical documentation
- Well-organized personal workspace for all artifacts
- Beautiful presentations that users are proud to share
- Privacy-first storage (default private, opt-in sharing)

### Out of Scope for MVP

**Agent Marketplace**
- Start with curated, pre-configured agents instead of vast marketplace
- Defer community-contributed and industry-specific specialists

**Custom Agent Creation**
- Users get organizational + personal agents, but skip "build your own" complexity
- Focus on perfecting existing agent experiences first

**Advanced RBAC and Enterprise Features**
- Basic permissions (org/team/personal) sufficient for MVP
- Defer enterprise-grade governance, audit trails, compliance dashboards

**Complex Integrations**
- Start with Google Calendar (read-only) and basic JIRA integration
- Defer two-way calendar updates, Slack bots, GitHub integration
- Focus on one meeting platform perfectly vs. multiple platforms

**Advanced Artifact Management**
- Simple artifact storage and organization
- Defer complex sharing workflows, advanced templates, expiration policies
